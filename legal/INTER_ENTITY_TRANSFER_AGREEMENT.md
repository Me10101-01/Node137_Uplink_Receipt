## ARTICLE I: TRANSFER OBLIGATION

### 1.1 Irrevocable Assignment
The Transferor irrevocably assigns and commits to transfer **seven percent (7%)** of all gross revenue received by Strategickhaos DAO LLC to ValorYield Engine on a monthly basis.

### 1.2 Revenue Definition
For purposes of this Agreement, "gross revenue" includes but is not limited to:
- Donations received via cryptocurrency wallets
- NFT sales and royalties
- Smart contract fees and transaction proceeds
- Licensing fees for patented technology
- Service fees from UIDP (Universal Identity Donation Protocol) transactions
- Any other income generated by DAO operations

### 1.3 Revenue Exclusions
The following are excluded from gross revenue calculation:
- Pass-through transactions where the DAO acts solely as a custodian
- Refunds or chargebacks
- Inter-entity test transactions

## ARTICLE II: CHARITABLE PURPOSE

### 2.1 Mission Alignment
Transfers to ValorYield Engine serve the following charitable purposes:
- Medical research support (St. Jude Children's Research Hospital)
- International humanitarian aid (Médecins Sans Frontières/Doctors Without Frontiers)
- Veteran support organizations
- Educational initiatives in cybersecurity and AI ethics
- Open-source technology development for public benefit

### 2.2 Beneficiary Restrictions
ValorYield Engine shall distribute funds only to IRS-recognized 501(c)(3) organizations or equivalent international charitable entities.

## ARTICLE III: EXECUTION MECHANISM

### 3.1 Algorithmic Implementation
The 7% transfer shall be executed algorithmically through smart contract logic and/or automated financial systems, ensuring:
- Real-time calculation of the 7% allocation
- Immediate routing to ValorYield Engine designated accounts
- Immutable transaction logging on blockchain
- No human override capability (except for emergency security measures)

### 3.2 Technical Implementation
The transfer mechanism operates via:
- UIDP webhook handlers (Stripe, PayPal, cryptocurrency)
- Smart contract escrow functions
- Automated accounting reconciliation
- Monthly verification and reporting

### 3.3 Transparency Requirements
All transfers shall be:
- Publicly logged on blockchain with transaction hashes
- Recorded in monthly transparency reports
- Available for audit by any party
- Timestamped using OpenTimestamps or equivalent

## ARTICLE IV: GOVERNANCE

### 4.1 No Override Authority
Neither party may unilaterally modify or suspend the 7% transfer obligation without:
- Mutual written consent of both entities
- Public notice period of minimum 90 days
- Documentation of extraordinary circumstances requiring modification

### 4.2 Emergency Provisions
The only permissible temporary suspension circumstances are:
- Security breach requiring immediate system lockdown
- Legal compliance requirements mandating cessation
- Technical failure preventing safe execution
- Force majeure events

### 4.3 Restoration Obligation
Any suspended transfers must be:
- Restored within 30 days of suspension cause resolution
- Retroactively calculated and executed for the suspension period
- Publicly reported with detailed explanation

## ARTICLE V: COMPLIANCE AND REPORTING

### 5.1 Monthly Reconciliation
By the 10th day of each month, both entities shall:
- Verify previous month's gross revenue
- Confirm 7% transfer amount and execution
- Publish reconciliation report to public repository
- Archive all transaction records

### 5.2 Annual Audit
Both entities commit to:
- Annual financial review by independent third party
- Publication of audit results (with appropriate privacy protections)
- Correction of any discrepancies within 60 days

### 5.3 Public Accessibility
All reports shall be published to:
- GitHub repository: https://github.com/Me10101-01/Node137_Uplink_Receipt
- IPFS for permanent archival
- Arweave for immutable storage
- Social media for broad accessibility

## ARTICLE VI: AMENDMENT AND TERMINATION

### 6.1 Amendment Process
This Agreement may only be amended by:
1. Written proposal from either party
2. 90-day public comment period
3. Mutual consent documented in signed writing
4. Publication of amended agreement to all public channels

### 6.2 Termination Conditions
This Agreement may only be terminated upon:
- Dissolution of either entity per applicable law
- Mutual written consent after public notice period
- Court order from competent jurisdiction
- Completion of mission (charitable purpose fulfilled)

### 6.3 Survival Clauses
Upon termination, obligations survive for:
- All accrued but unpaid transfers
- Completion of current fiscal period transfers
- Final reconciliation and audit
- Archive maintenance of all historical records
