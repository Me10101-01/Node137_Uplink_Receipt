# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Effective Date**: January 1, 2025

**Between**: Data Controller ("Controller") and Data Processor ("Processor")

---

## 1. Definitions

- **Personal Data**: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Processing**: Any operation performed on Personal Data
- **Data Subject**: The individual to whom Personal Data relates

## 2. Processing of Personal Data

### 2.1 Processor Obligations

Processor shall:

1. Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Controller
2. Ensure persons authorized to process Personal Data have committed to confidentiality
3. Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
4. Assist Controller in responding to Data Subject requests
5. Delete or return all Personal Data after termination

### 2.2 Security Measures

Processor shall implement measures including:

- Pseudonymization and encryption
- Ensuring confidentiality, integrity, availability
- Regular testing and evaluation of effectiveness
- Incident response procedures

## 3. Sub-Processors

Processor may engage sub-processors only with prior written consent from Controller.

Processor must:
- Conduct due diligence on sub-processors
- Impose same data protection obligations
- Remain fully liable for sub-processor's acts

## 4. Data Subject Rights

Processor shall assist Controller in:

- Responding to data subject access requests
- Data rectification and erasure requests
- Data portability requests
- Objections to processing

Response time: Within 30 days of receipt

## 5. Data Breaches

In event of Personal Data breach, Processor shall:

1. Notify Controller without undue delay (max 72 hours)
2. Provide details of the breach
3. Describe measures taken to address the breach
4. Cooperate with Controller's breach response

## 6. Audits and Inspections

Controller may audit Processor's compliance:

- With reasonable notice (14 days minimum)
- During normal business hours
- At Controller's expense

Processor must provide all information necessary to demonstrate compliance.

## 7. International Transfers

Personal Data shall not be transferred outside the EEA without:

- Adequate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses)
- Prior written authorization from Controller
- Compliance with applicable data protection laws

## 8. Term and Termination

This Agreement commences on Effective Date and continues until termination of the underlying service agreement.

Upon termination, Processor shall within 30 days:
- Return all Personal Data to Controller
- Delete all copies (unless retention required by law)
- Certify destruction in writing

## 9. Liability and Indemnification

Processor shall indemnify Controller against all claims arising from:

- Processor's breach of this Agreement
- Processor's violation of data protection laws
- Unauthorized processing or disclosure

Liability cap: $1,000,000 per incident

## 10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by GDPR and laws of the European Union.
